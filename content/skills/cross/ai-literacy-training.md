Literacy is not **one Zoom recording**. It’s **layered enablement** matched to risk and leverage: everyone gets **core concepts**; high-impact roles get **deep labs**.

**Core (all employees)**: What models *are*, **limits**, **data handling rules**, **secret policies**, and **escalation paths** for odd outputs. Include **30-minute hands-on** with approved tool in sandbox data.

**IC tracks**: Engineers learn **test-first prompting**, **diff review**, and **supply-chain vigilance** for dependencies. Designers learn **system adherence** and **a11y checks**. PMs learn **evidence discipline** and **spec completeness**.

**Managers**: Train on **measuring outcomes vs output**, **spotting burnout from tool pressure**, and **fair performance evaluation** when AI assists work product.

**Leaders**: Short **strategy module** on **build vs buy**, **vendor risk**, and **regulatory trends**. Align on **principles** publicly so teams don’t guess from memos.

**Continuous**: Monthly **tips digest**, internal **FAQ**, and **office hours**. Update curriculum when **major model or policy changes** hit.

**Assessment**: Lightweight **completion checks**, not ideological quizzes. Optional **advanced certifications** for support and sales facing customers.

**Ethics & inclusion**: Teach **bias awareness** and **accessibility expectations**. Literacy without ethics **scales harm faster**.
