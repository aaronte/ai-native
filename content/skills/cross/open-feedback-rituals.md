Fast shipping without feedback devolves into **silent resentment** and **wrong priors**. Build **high-frequency, low-drama** feedback loops tied to **work artifacts**, not personality windmills.

**Principles**: **Situation–behavior–impact** framing; **kindness + clarity**; **assume positive intent** first pass; **document commitments** after hard feedback. Separate **code critique** from **personal critique** explicitly in review norms.

**Rituals**: Managers hold **weekly 1:1s** focused on blockers and growth, not status. Run **quarterly cross-functional feedback** (short survey + 3 open prompts). For leaders, run **skip-levels** twice yearly—signals bubble early.

**Peer**: Encourage **request-for-feedback** notes on PRs and specs (“What am I missing?”). Time-box responses. Recognize **useful dissent** publicly.

**AI etiquette**: Don’t use models to **ghostwrite harsh feedback**—people detect it and trust drops. OK to **draft a structure**; human must **own tone and facts**.

**Repairs**: When feedback misfires, **acknowledge quickly** and reset expectations. Track **repeat themes** in performance systems; surprises at review time mean **rituals failed**.

**Metrics**: Healthy teams show **rising psychological safety scores** (if surveyed) and **decreasing passive-aggressive chat**. Proxy measures include **shorter time from issue raised to resolution**.
