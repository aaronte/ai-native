AI raises **individual output**; without transparency, teams **duplicate work** and **lose context**. Default to **working out loud** in durable systems, not ephemeral chat.

**Norms**: Prefer **2-minute Looms with AI summaries** over written status or 30-minute meetings for context transfer. Route the AI summary, transcript, and decisions into the wiki/ticket automatically so future searchers find the source and the compressed version. Written summaries are the fallback, not the default.

**Building in public (internal)**: Lightweight **demo Fridays**, **#ship** channel with screenshots, and **monthly engineering/design spotlight**. Celebrate **learnings**, not only launches.

**Meetings**: Replace agenda/pre-read homework with an **auto-generated meeting brief** from tickets, PRs, docs, and prior decisions. Kill recurring meetings that **don’t change decisions**. Replace status with **dashboards and bot digests**. For cross-timezone teams, **rotate meeting pain**—don’t freeze one region at 6am forever.

**Documentation debt**: Treat missing docs as **bugs**. Have AI draft docs from Loom transcripts, merged PRs, accepted designs, and incident notes; humans approve accuracy. Replace static onboarding checklists with an onboarding bot that links the right docs and asks for one-click feedback when something is stale.

**Trust**: Transparency reduces **politics**—assumptions surface faster. Leaders **admit mistakes** on strategy shifts; teams reciprocate with **early risk flags**.

**Boundaries**: Transparency isn’t **oversharing personal data** or **leaking customer info**. Publish **redaction guidelines** for recordings and transcripts.
