AI accelerates **transcription, tagging, and pattern spotting** across calls, tickets, session replays, win/loss notes, and surveys—it does not replace **choosing which customers represent your future revenue**. Start every discovery cycle with a **hypothesis and segment definition**, then let the system assemble the evidence packet.

**Signal loops**: Run **weekly** (not quarterly) thin slices of research from recorded calls, support tickets, session replays, Slack threads, and sales notes routed to a theme. Use AI to **cluster quotes**, clip moments, and suggest **contradictions** to investigate. Always **trace claims** back to primary sources—summaries lie when incentives are messy.

**Humans in the loop**: PMs must **talk to users** on hard problems—pricing, positioning, churn drivers. Use AI for **prep** (question banks, competitor scans) and **draft summaries**, not for **substituting** stakeholder judgment. Flag when your training data is **mostly vocal power users**; seek **silent churned accounts** via outbound or win-loss partners.

**Artifacts**: Maintain a **living evidence log**: date, segment, method, key quote,/decision link. AI can help **maintain links** between insights and roadmap items. Avoid one-off slide decks that rot.

**Operational excellence**: Time-box synthesis; **ship decisions** even when evidence is partial. Publish **what would change our mind** criteria alongside bets. Discovery speed only matters if **exec alignment** and **engineering capacity** match—otherwise you’re generating ideas you cannot ship.
